The real world problem I decided to look at was the amount of police incidents near my apartment in chapel hill. To create this map, I found a vector shapefile of points where there were police incidents in Chapel Hill since 2010. I then created a new point layer where I designated a point as my apartment. After, I created a buffer around my apartment of 1.5 miles to see how many incidents have occured. I then performed and intersection method to find the exact number of incidents in my proximity. This number came out to be 50,118. This problem was interesting to me because I wanted to see how dangerous the sorrounding area actually is to me and my friends. A high number of 50k causes a lot to be concerned about. It may be interesting for the future. to see how many of these incidents turned into arrests or how many were violent confrontations.
